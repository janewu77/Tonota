Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🤫

100% on-device, zero cloud

All transcription runs locally using WhisperKit and Apple's Neural Engine. Your audio and text never touch a server — no account, no tracking, works offline.

⚡️

Start recording in one tapv1.6

Long-press the app icon for a quick-action shortcut, or say "Hey Siri, start recording with Tonota" — no unlocking, no navigating, just speak.

🔒

Lock screen & Control Centerv1.6

Add a Tonota button to your lock screen or Control Center. One tap starts recording instantly — even before you unlock your phone.

▶️

Play back originals

Every recording is kept. Tap play in the detail view to hear the original audio any time — perfect for double-checking a tricky word.

🔍

Search everything

Tap search in any list to filter instantly by title and transcript content — so the memo you need is always a few keystrokes away.

🗂️

Folders & organization

Sort recordings into your own folders and browse by collection. Keep your ideas, meetings, and notes cleanly separated.

✅

Bulk move & deletev1.6

Multi-select recordings to move them to any folder or delete them all at once. Keep your library tidy without touching each memo one by one.

📁

Export text + audio

Turn on export in Settings and Tonota saves each transcript as a .txt file bundled with the original audio recording to any folder you choose.

🌍

10 languages, auto-detected

English, Chinese, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese and more. WhisperKit detects your language automatically — or pin one in Settings.
