Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026 · v1.4

Overview

Tonota does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. All audio recordings and transcriptions remain on your device at all times.

What data Tonota accesses

DataPurposeLeaves your device?
Microphone audioRecording voice memosNo
Transcription textGenerated locally, stored in SwiftDataNo
Audio filesStored locally in the app sandboxNo
Folder namesOptional labels you create to organize memos, stored locallyNo
Optional export folderTranscript .txt files and their original audio recordings, written to a folder of your choice on your device or iCloud DriveNo

If you choose an export folder that is synced by iCloud Drive (or another file provider you have set up), those files will be handled by that service under its own privacy terms. Tonota itself performs no upload — it only writes files to the location you pick.

What Tonota does NOT do

  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No analytics or crash reporting to third parties
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No network requests after the initial model download
  • No data shared with Apple or any other party beyond what iOS itself collects

Third-party dependencies

WhisperKit (open-source, MIT licence) runs entirely on-device using Apple's Core ML framework. WhisperKit does not make network calls during inference. Model weights are downloaded once from Hugging Face CDN on first setup; no personal data is included in that request.

Your rights

Because Tonota collects no personal data, there is nothing to delete, export, or correct. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored recordings and transcriptions.
